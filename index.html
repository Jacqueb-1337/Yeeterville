Break Rules

In Yeeterville, there are many rules that are set in place to retain order, but you may look danger in the eyes and defy the laws by joining the rebellion and fighting back for your rights.

Be who you want to be

Choose your character! Each character is custom tailored to their players for the best experience. Any traits or backstory you'd like your character to have, just ask Jacqueb.

Overthrow the Government

In these trying times, Yeeterville has fallen into the hands of a leader who is a bit too harsh and abuses his power. The rebellion is slowly building its strength and forces to eventually take on the government and free the land from a terrible fascist dictator.
